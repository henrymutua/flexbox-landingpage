Random architecture facts

Modernism is defined by buildings that range in date from the mid- 1950s to the mid-1970s, although many contemporary buildings are constructed in the modernist style as well.

The first major element of modernism is the idea of volume versus mass.

Most traditional buildings constructed before the Modernist period were adorned with classically influenced ornamentation such as columns and friezes.

With modernism, symmetry gives way to regularity, which can be seen in the regular lines of windows.
